About the Platform

AI Platform Albania is being built to address a growing gap in the Albanian market: strong interest in artificial intelligence, but limited access to practical, structured, and locally relevant learning solutions.

The platform is designed to help users move beyond curiosity and into real use. Instead of offering only general information, it combines readiness assessment, guided learning paths, practical resources, and progress tracking in one place.

Our goal is to make AI easier to understand, easier to apply, and more useful for youth, SMEs, and education-focused users across Albania.
